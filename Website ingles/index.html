Our Process

How is your furniture made?

Before reaching your home, each piece goes through several carefully executed stages — from sourcing raw materials to protective packaging designed to ensure safe delivery. This journey can be divided into eight essential steps.

Cutting stage

Everything begins with the cutting stage, where each piece is produced according to the product’s design. It is here that the furniture moves from concept to form, crafted with precision and strict adherence to defined measurements.

Edge-banding stage

After cutting, the pieces move to the edge-banding stage, where the edges are carefully sealed. This process adds protection, durability, and a refined, uniform finish to the furniture.

Drilling Stage

Drilling Stage: All necessary holes and markings are made to ensure proper assembly. This process guarantees precise fittings and facilitates the final assembly.

Sanding stage

Next, the pieces go through the sanding process, where imperfections are removed. This step ensures a smooth surface prepared for painting, elevating the standard of the final finish.

Pre-Assembly stage

Before painting, the pieces go through a pre-assembly stage to check fittings and alignment. This step ensures that all components are correct and ready for the next stages of the process.

Painting stage

The pieces go through a standardized painting process, with multiple layers applied to ensure resistance and durability. After this stage, each piece is carefully inspected, and if any detail falls outside the standard, it is returned for correction to guarantee consistent quality.

Sorting stage

After painting, the pieces are organized according to each product’s technical specifications. Before packaging, a final check is performed to ensure every component is correct and complete.

Packaging stage

In the final stage, each product is individually packaged using protective materials and corner guards to prevent movement during transport, ensuring the furniture arrives in perfect condition.

NOTE: Our packaging standards follow international norms and practices adopted by several countries, ensuring maximum protection, transport safety, and preservation of product quality from manufacturing to final delivery.

Who we are

Mambel Móveis Ltda. has been operating in the furniture industry since 1993, specializing in the development and manufacturing of bedroom furniture. With over 30 years of experience, our work is reflected in quality, design, and impeccable finishing. Founded in Lagoa Vermelha, Mambel was created with the purpose of producing furniture that combines functionality, durability, and aesthetics, while keeping pace with market trends and the needs of each customer. We continuously invest in technology, production processes, and design to deliver reliable and modern products. Today, we serve both national and international markets, offering solutions that transform bedrooms into unique, comfortable environments full of personality — the essence of our slogan: “Furniture with a personality of its own” Mambel Móveis creates more than furniture: we create spaces with identity.
